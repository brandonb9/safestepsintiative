SafeSteps Initiative

Advocating for a safer student crossing near Jose Marti MAST Academy through research, community outreach, surveys, interviews, and data collection.

Share Your Experience Learn About the Project

Helping students and families cross with greater safety.

SafeSteps Initiative is a student-led project dedicated to improving crossing safety for students and families near Jose Marti MAST Academy.

For years, students and families have expressed concerns about crossing conditions near our school. Every day, students and families cross between the school and the shopping center area, often in locations that were not designed to safely accommodate the current volume of foot traffic during arrival and dismissal.

Our mission is to better understand these safety concerns, document how the issue impacts our community, and support efforts that create safer crossing options for students.

Student-led Created to center student voices in local safety conversations.
Community-focused Built around input from students, parents, staff, and neighbors.
Data-informed Guided by surveys, interviews, traffic observations, and research.

How We Are Building the Case

SafeSteps is collecting meaningful data and real experiences so school and community leaders can better understand what students face during arrival and dismissal.

1

Survey Responses

Gathering input from students, parents, staff, and community members about crossing concerns.

2

Interviews

Documenting personal experiences to show how the issue affects daily travel to and from school.

3

Traffic Observations

Studying crossing patterns and foot traffic during key arrival and dismissal times.

4

Collaboration

Working with administrators, community leaders, and local officials to identify possible solutions.
